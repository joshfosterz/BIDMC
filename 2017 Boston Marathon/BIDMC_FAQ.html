Offline Donations

    An offline donation is a gift in support of your run that is not made on Crowdrise. These gifts will be processed by the BIDMC Office of Development and included on a weekly report sent to all runners every Friday. It is your responsibility to review this report and enter any new offline donations received so that they may be counted in your total:

    • Checks made payable to Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center or BIDMC with “runner’s name / Boston Marathon 2017” in the memo line can be sent to: Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, Office of Development, 330 Brookline Avenue, Boston, MA 02215.
    • Cash gifts can be submitted in one of two ways:

        • By mail Please use the cash to purchase a money order or bank check and send to BIDMC using the check instructions above.

        In person Please contact Amy Fisher at ajfisher@bidmc.harvard.edu to schedule a time to meet at either 20 Overland Street or 330 Brookline Avenue in Boston to submit the donations.

    • Please contact Catherine Halpin at chalpin@bidmc.harvard.edu or 617-667-3393 regarding gifts by wire transfers, stock or from a donor advised charitable gift account.

    • Matching gifts are a great way to increase your fundraising total. Be sure to ask if your employer has a matching gift program. Also ask people within your network if their company has a matching gift program.

      All matching gifts will be automatically be applied to the fundraising totals of the same runner the original gift was made to. Matching gifts will appear on the offline donations report once received by BIDMC. Forms provided by the donor’s employer can be mailed to the following address:

      Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center
      Office of Development
      330 Brookline Ave (OV)
      Boston, MA 02215

How to Add Offline Donations to Crowdrise

  1. Visit treadstrong.org and log into your Crowdrise account
  2. Click the ‘Manage Campaign’ tab
  3. Click ‘Advanced’ on the menu at left and select ‘Offline Donation Manager’ from the options
  4. Enter offline donations using the fields provided, and select ‘Submit’
