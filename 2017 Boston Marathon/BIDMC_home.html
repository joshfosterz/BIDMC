A message from our Team Captain

Hello BIDMC marathon team! I am incredibly honored to be the honorary captain of our team. The commitment you all have made to this world class medical center is a gift that will yield returns for patients and their families. I am one of those fortunate patients and my family and I owe a lifetime debt to all of the wonderful clinicians at BID. As you embark on this unforgettable journey that will culminate on Boylston Street, know that I will be your biggest cheerleader. The Boston Marathon has become an irreplaceable piece of my biography, and it will soon become a part of yours as well. Best of luck and you'll be hearing from me again soon. Boston Strong. – Patrick
