How to Create Your Crowdrise Page

  1. Visit treadstrong.org
  2. Click the ‘Fundraise for this Campaign’ button (along the right side of the screen)
  3. Sign into your existing Crowdrise account or sign up for a new account
  4. Accept the $7,500 fundraising minimum and enter your credit card information
  5. Build your personal fundraising page!
    a. Upload your photo(s)
    b. Write a compelling “Why I am running” story, make sure to include the fund
    c. Set your fundraising goal for $10,000 (while this is not the minimum, it’s what you should aim for … or more!)
  6. Edit your story and/or photo gallery during the training season as needed. Use first names only if you are sharing other people’s stories and get their permission first.

Fundraising Tips
  • Share the URL for your personal fundraising page with potential supporters. Individuals can also visit treadstrong.org and select your profile from the list of team members
  • Ask using multiple channels to spread the word about what you are doing (snail mail, email, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
  • Ask again and get creative about how you’re doing it. If you’ve already explained your story and your mission, start sending updates about training milestones.
  • It’s not about you! It’s about the amazing program you are supporting. Keep this in mind when you’re hesitant to ask again.
  • Take advantage of all your resources, especially friends and family members who can act as your champions and also fundraise within their own networks on your behalf.
  • Have fun and be creative with your fundraising methods. One past runner baked brownies for anyone who donated more than a certain amount on Mondays. Other runners have approached businesses (restaurants, Spin and Yogo studios, the paint bar) about donating a percent of their proceeds.
